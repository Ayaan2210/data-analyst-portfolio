
# Global Superstore Sales & Customer Analysis (SQL)

## 📌 Objective
To analyze sales, profit, customers, and product performance using SQL and generate meaningful business insights.

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### 🔹 Overall Business Performance
- Total Sales, Total Profit, and Profit Margin calculation

### 🔹 Category Analysis
- Category-wise sales and profit comparison
- Profit margin by category

### 🔹 Time-Based Analysis
- Monthly sales trend
- Month-over-Month (MoM) growth %
- Best performing month

### 🔹 Customer Analysis
- Total sales per customer
- Top 5 customers
- Customer segmentation (High, Medium, Low value)

### 🔹 Product Analysis
- Top 5 best-selling products
- Identification of loss-making products

### 🔹 Regional Analysis
- Sales by region
- Most profitable region
- Least profitable region

---

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques Used
- SQL (PostgreSQL / MySQL)
- CTEs (Common Table Expressions)
- Window Functions (LAG, ROW_NUMBER)
- Aggregations & Grouping
- CASE Statements

---

## 📁 Dataset
- Global Superstore dataset  
(Contains orders, customers, products, sales, and profit data)

---

## 📌 Key Insights
- Certain categories contribute significantly more to total revenue
- Sales show a consistent trend over months with noticeable growth periods
- A small group of customers generates a large portion of total sales
- Some products are consistently loss-making and need review
- Regional performance varies significantly, indicating optimization opportunities

---

- Data Analysis using SQL
- Business Insight Generation
- Data Aggregation & Transformation
- Analytical Thinking
